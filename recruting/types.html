We are more than pottery making

Becuase we are a growing company, it is natural that we recruite people that focus on other essential services in our company. These often include the sales team, and the school team. The sales team main focus is on advertizments and keeping in touch with cliants both home and abroad. the school team main focus is to teach classes and to down orders from clinents on other assingments and to pass them on to the head manager of the craftsmen who then assigne a craftsmen to complete those orders.

The School Team

The school team main focus is to teach classes to both children and adults on how to make pottery by hand. These classes are desinged to teach both hobbyists and enthuseates on how to create works of art and how to keep the craftsmen alive in pottery making. They are also incharge of quality contorl and they make sure the products made by the craftsment are up to the standards of Hulma pottery.

The Sales team

The sales team main responsibilities is to find clients to sell our mechendise to and they are also the ones in charge of advertizments and and attending events such as farmers markets and fairs to gian more clients and to gain a better foothold in the market
